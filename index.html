    • I’m a 2nd year PhD student at Humboldt University of Berlin and MDC Berlin. My supervisors are Ulf Leser and Jana Wolf. I am interested in developing Machine Learning methods for extracting structured information from text. I am doing this mostly in the biomedical domain to build tools that may help to make sense of the enormous amount of research publications. I apply Machine Learning also to other problems in Biology and I am interested in approaches for computational reasoning.

    Before my starting my PhD, I completed my MSc and BSc in Computer Science at Humboldt University and Free University in Berlin. I also hold a BA in Philosophy from Bamberg University.
