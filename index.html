    • About me

    I’m a 2nd year PhD student at Humboldt University of Berlin and MDC Berlin. My supervisors are Ulf Leser and Jana Wolf. In my research, I am mainly interested in developing Machine Learning methods for extracting structured information from text. I am doing this mainly in the biomedical domain to build tools that may help to make sense of the enormous amount of research publications. I am also interested in applying Machine Learning to other problems in Biology and in approaches for computational reasoning.

    Before my starting my PhD, I completed my MSc and BSc in Computer Science at Humboldt University and Free University in Berlin. I also hold a BA in Philosophy from Bamberg University.
